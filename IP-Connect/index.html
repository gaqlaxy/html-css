What is IP Connect

IP Connect is ICMEG's intellectual property marketplace for India's screen industry, enabling creators, producers and platforms to trade rights and coordinate deals.

Through our three pillars — the Buyer-Seller Market, Pitch2Deal, and the Script Room — we streamline licensing, curate group pitches, and offer professional script consulting and IP registration in one unified platform.
